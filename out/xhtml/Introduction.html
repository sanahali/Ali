What is the Cuisinart ICE-60W?

The Cuisinart ICE-60W is a top of the line machine that can make ice cream, frozen yogurt, gelato, or sorbet; it processes your mixture in 20 minutes! All of the attachments that you need to concoct your ice cream creation will be at the ice cream station in Shanks.
